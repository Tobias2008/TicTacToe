How does this Website work?

This Website is mainly about the Tic Tac Toe on the page "Start Game". There you can play Against a friend or your personal enemy! For every win you get +1 Score. You can buy things in the Store like an better Character or a cooler Color! But there are many more things! Just take a look an go on the "Store" page! And at the last there is a page that is called "Settings" where you can choose the things you bought. At the beginning there wont be much but if you grind enough with your friends, you can reach everthing and get a full inventory!

Much fun playing!
